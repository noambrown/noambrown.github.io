--- layout: home title: Ellen Vitercik subtitle: vitercik@stanford.edu --- I am a Miller fellow at UC Berkeley, where I am hosted by Michael Jordan and Jennifer Chayes. In Fall 2022, I will join Stanford as an Assistant Professor with a joint appointment between the Management Science and Engineering department and the Computer Science department. My research revolves around machine learning theory, algorithm design, and the interface between economics and computation.

I received a PhD in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon University, where I was advised by Nina Balcan and Tuomas Sandholm. My thesis won the SIGecom Doctoral Dissertation Award, the CMU School of Computer Science Distinguished Dissertation Award, and the Honorable Mention Victor Lesser Distinguished Dissertation Award.

I pronounce my last name VIH-ter-sik.
